About Kid Rock

                

                     Robert James Ritchie (born January 17, 1971), most famously known as Kid Rock, is an American singer, songwriter, and rapper. His musical style is a blend of rock, hip hop, country, and heavy metal.                

                

                     Emerging from the Detroit music scene, Kid Rock gained mainstream success with his 1998 album "Devil Without a Cause," which sold 14 million copies worldwide. He is known for his dynamic live performances and genre-bending sound.
